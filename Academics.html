Academics

At RGHS we offer a comprehensive academic program that is designed to prepare our students for success in their future endeavors.

At Royal Giant High School, we offer a comprehensive academic program that is designed to prepare our students for success in their future endeavors.
Our curriculum is based on the Ugandan national education system and includes a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, literature, social studies, and foreign languages.

We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities and clubs to help our students develop their interests and talents outside of the classroom.
These activities include sports teams, music and drama clubs, debate teams, and community service organizations.

Teaching Approach

We use a learner-centered approach with practical lessons, group discussions, and continuous assessments

Assessment

Student are evaluated through tests, mock exams final examinations.
