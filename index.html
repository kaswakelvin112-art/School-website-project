Royal Giant High School is a prestigious educational institution located in Mityana, Uganda. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for our students to thrive academically and personally.

Our History

Royal Giant High School was founded in 1996 by the late Reverend Dr. Johnson Muyiwa, who was a prominent figure in Ugandan society during the 1990s. The school was established to provide a safe and supportive environment for young people to grow and develop their talents.

Over the years, the school has grown and evolved, becoming a leading institution in the Ugandan education system. Today, it is a respected institution that prepares students for success in their future endeavors.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Integrity is a core value at Royal Giant High School. We believe in upholding the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and responsibility in all aspects of our operations. We expect our students to always act with integrity and to uphold the values of honesty, respect, and fairness.

Respect

Respect is another core value at Royal Giant High School. We believe in treating all individuals with respect and dignity, regardless of their background, beliefs, or circumstances. We expect our students to always treat others with kindness, empathy, and understanding.

Excellence

Excellence is a key value at Royal Giant High School. We believe in striving for excellence in all aspects of our operations, from academics to extracurricular activities. We expect our students to always strive for excellence and to work hard to achieve their full potential.

God-Fearing

God-Fearing is a core value at Royal Giant High School. We believe in fostering a sense of spirituality and reverence for a higher power among our students. We encourage our students to develop a strong moral compass and to live their lives with integrity, compassion, and respect for others.

Our Academic Programs

At Royal Giant High School, we offer a comprehensive academic program that is designed to prepare our students for success in their future endeavors. Our curriculum is based on the Ugandan national education system and includes a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, literature, social studies, and foreign languages.

We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities and clubs to help our students develop their interests and talents outside of the classroom. These activities include sports teams, music and drama clubs, debate teams, and community service organizations.
