Welcome to Royal Giant High School

Who we are

The Center of Excellence

Royal Giant High School is a prestigious educational institution located in Mityana, Uganda. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for our students to thrive academically and personally.

Our school offers a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and sports to ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education. We believe in empowering our students to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

Giving quality education, moral guidance and contemporary life skills to all learners.

Our Vision

To Position Royal Giant High School a Centre of Excellence

Our Academic Programs

At Royal Giant High School, we offer a comprehensive academic program that is designed to prepare our students for success in their future endeavors. Our curriculum is based on the Ugandan national education system and includes a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, literature, social studies, and foreign languages.

We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities and clubs to help our students develop their interests and talents outside of the classroom. These activities include sports teams, music and drama clubs, debate teams, and community service organizations.
